📊 Project Summary

This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales performance using a dataset containing **5,009 orders**, generating **$2.30M in total sales** and **$286.4K in total profit**, with an overall **profit margin of 12.47%** and an **average discount of 15.62%**.

---

🔍 Key Insights

💰 Sales & Profit Overview

* Total Sales: **$2.30M**
* Total Profit: **$286.4K**
* Profit Margin: **12.47%**
* Total Orders: **5,009**

---

📦 Category Performance

* **Technology** is the most profitable category (**$145K profit**)
* **Office Supplies** also performs well (**$122K profit**)
* **Furniture** has very low profit (**$18K**) and is the weakest category

---

🛍 Sub-Category Insights

* Top profit-generating sub-categories:

  * Copiers (**$56K**)
  * Phones (**$45K**)
  * Accessories (**$42K**)

* Loss-making sub-categories:

  * Tables (**-$18K**)
  * Bookcases (**-$3K**)
  * Supplies (**-$1K**)

---

🎯 Segment Analysis

* **Consumer segment** contributes the highest profit (**46.8%**) and sales (**$1.16M**)
* Followed by:

  * Corporate (**$0.71M sales**)
  * Home Office (**$0.43M sales**)

---

📉 Discount Impact on Profit

* High discounts result in **significant losses across all categories**
* Total loss from high discount: **-$97K**
* Low discount levels generate the highest profit (**$421K**)

Indicates that **discount strategy directly impacts profitability**

---

🏆 Product Performance

* Top-performing product:

* Logitech P710e (**$11.2K sales**)
* Other top products contribute significantly but drop sharply after top 3

---

⚠️ Business Problems Identified

* High discounts are reducing overall profitability
* Furniture category is underperforming
* Certain sub-categories generate losses despite sales

---

✅ Recommendations

* Reduce heavy discounts, especially in Furniture
* Focus on high-margin categories like Technology
* Re-evaluate pricing strategy for loss-making sub-categories
* Promote top-performing products to maximize revenue

---

📌 Conclusion

The analysis highlights that while the business generates strong sales, **profitability is impacted by discounting strategies and underperforming categories**. Optimizing discounts and focusing on high-performing segments can significantly improve overall profit.
